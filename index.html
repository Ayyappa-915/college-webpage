ST.ANNS COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY:-
SACET STRUCTURE:

College Intro: St. Ann's College of Engineering & Technology (SACET) was established in 2001 in Chirala, Andhra Pradesh, India. It is a private institution that is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada. The college is known for its commitment to providing quality technical education to its students. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. Highlights: The college is known for its excellent infrastructure, experienced faculty, and placement record. It is also recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). Academics Courses: The college offers B.Tech in Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. It also offers M.Tech in various specializations. Duration and mode of each course: All courses are full-time. B.Tech is a 4-year program and M.Tech is a 2-year program. Admission Process: Eligibility criteria: For B.Tech, candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. For M.Tech, candidates must have a B.Tech degree in the relevant field. Application process and deadlines: Applications are accepted online through the official website.
